# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics
### Bridging the Digital Brain and the Physical Body

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook. This comprehensive resource guides you through the complexities of building, simulating, and controlling humanoid robots using modern AI techniques.

## Course Overview

This textbook is designed for advanced students and practitioners. It covers the full stack from high-speed actuation to high-level cognitive planning with Large Language Models.

## Getting Started

To get the most out of this book, you will need:
*   **Hardware**: A Linux PC with an NVIDIA GPU (RTX recommended), or access to cloud compute.
*   **Software**: ROS 2 (Humble or Jazzy), Python 3.10+, and Docker.
*   **Math**: Basic Linear Algebra (matrices/vectors) and Calculus.

Select a chapter from the sidebar to begin your journey into the future of robotics.
